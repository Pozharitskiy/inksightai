Privacy Policy

InkSight AI – Tattoo Ideas
Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

InkSight AI does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app does not require account registration.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API – used to generate tattoo design prompts and images. Prompts are sent to OpenAI servers for processing. See OpenAI Privacy Policy.
  • Pinterest API – used to publish generated images to Pinterest. See Pinterest Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

No user data is stored on our servers. Generated images are temporarily processed in memory and not retained after posting.

4. Cookies

This app does not use cookies.

5. Children's Privacy

InkSight AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
